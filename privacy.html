Privacy Policy for SenseAudio

Last Updated: January 2026

At Sensement Music, we value your creativity and privacy. This policy explains how we handle data within the SenseAudio extension and web application.

1

Data We Collect

SenseAudio is designed to be a "Privacy-First" tool. We do NOT collect:

  • Your Name, Email, or IP Address.
  • Your location or personal identity.
  • The content of your musical projects (unless explicitly shared/saved to cloud in future updates).

We DO collect anonymous usage statistics, such as:

App installation and startup.
Musical settings used (BPM, Key, Scale type).
Usage of AI generation features (Genre, Complexity).
Export actions (WAV/MIDI/JSON).
2

Why We Collect Data

We use this anonymous information solely to:

  • Improve the "MusicBrain" AI engine.
  • Understand which musical genres and features are most popular.
  • Fix technical bugs and optimize the Audio Engine performance.
3

Data Storage

All data is stored securely using Cloudflare D1 infrastructure. This data is private to Sensement Music and is never sold to third parties.
